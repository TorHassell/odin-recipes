Chili Recipe!

Below is a simpls 3 ingredient chili recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Turkey meat.
  • 1 16 oz can of kidney beans
  • 1 Jar of salsa (medium or spicy)
  1. Mist a wide pot or large skillet with nonstick oil spray and warm over medium-high heat.
  2. Brown meat for about 6 minutes, breaking it apart into crumbles as it cooks.
  3. Add beans and salsa and mix until everything is well-combined.
  4. Lower heat to medium and continue to cook for another 3 to 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until your chili is warmed through.
  5. Serve in bowls with toppings of choice.

and there you have it, Delicious Chili.
