Watermelon Pops.

Here I will show you basic 3 ingredient Watermelon Popsicles!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of watermelon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice

Steps

  1. Cut watermelon into small chunks, discarding the rind.
  2. Put the watermelon chunks into a blender with the sugar and lime juice.
  3. Process until smooth. Add enough water to make it liquefy
  4. Pour into popsicle molds, and freeze.
